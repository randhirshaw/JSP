Your Actionable Roadmap for a Data Analyst Role

Your Weekly Schedule

A focused 2-hour block each weekday to maximize your job search efforts.

3:00 - 4:00 PM

Networking & Outreach

Identify 5-10 people on LinkedIn in target roles. Personalize and send connection requests.

4:00 - 5:00 PM

Follow-Up & Scheduling

Follow up on previous requests. Schedule 15-minute informational interviews to learn and connect.

3:00 - 4:00 PM

Portfolio Development

Refine a project: write a blog post, enhance a Tableau dashboard, or clean up Python code.

4:00 - 5:00 PM

Skill Development

Practice SQL on HackerRank, take a Python tutorial, or learn a new Power BI feature.

3:00 - 4:00 PM

Active Job Search

Search for new openings on LinkedIn, Indeed, and company career pages that match your targets.

4:00 - 5:00 PM

Applications & Tracking

Apply to 3-5 jobs, customizing your resume/cover letter. Update your job search tracker.

3:00 - 4:00 PM

Behavioral Interview Prep

Practice answering common questions using the STAR method. Frame your stories effectively.

4:00 - 5:00 PM

Technical Interview Prep

Work on live SQL challenges on LeetCode or walk through a data analysis case study.

3:00 - 4:00 PM

Company Research

Research the data stack, culture, and recent news for your target companies to tailor applications.

4:00 - 5:00 PM

Weekly Review & Planning

Review your progress, update your tracker, and plan your activities for the upcoming week.
